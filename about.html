About Us

We are a group of high schoolers that realized that our local community projects needed more empowering to make thier work more impactful and our lives better.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide money to underfunded local initiatives that help to serve our community.

Money is a key aspect to not only our lives, but also the main requirement of many local projects that provide to the community through thier motives. Our goal is to help provide such local initiatives with the capital they need to continue making our community a better place.

We do this because we believe that our community is one of the most important things to us, as it gives us a safe sense of home and surrounds us in our day-to-day lives. As residents of our community, we decided to find a way to give back to it, through helping to fundraise impactful local initiatives.

Hence, we ask for your support in any way possible — be it through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word—so we can continue to empower local projects and make a lasting difference together.
