About Us

We are a group of high schoolers who realized that our local community projects needed more support to make their work more impactful and improve our lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide funding to underfunded local initiatives that help serve our community.

Money is not only a key part of our personal lives but also a major need for many local projects that improve our communities through their initiatives. Our goal is to help provide these efforts with the capital they need to keep making a difference.

We do this because we believe our community is one of the most important parts of our lives — it gives us a sense of home and is with us every day. Hence, we decided to give back by fundraising for impactful local initiatives.

That's why we ask for your support in any way you can — whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word — so we can continue to empower local projects and make a lasting difference together.

Latest Cause:



Partnering with TechOWL @ Temple University: the State Assistive Technology Act Program for the state of Pennsylvania. Based at the Institute on Disabilities at Temple University, they educate, inform, and empower Pennsylvanians across the state about assistive technology through technology demonstrations, an assistive technology lending library, equipment re-use programs, 3D printing, presentations, and other activities to help increase access to devices that help people do the activities they want and need to do in their lives.
