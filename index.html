Supporting Community Projects and Services

Latest Cause:



Partnering with TechOWL @ Temple University: the State Assistive Technology Act Program for the state of Pennsylvania. Based at the Institute on Disabilities at Temple University, they educate, inform, and empower Pennsylvanians across the state about assistive technology through technology demonstrations, an assistive technology lending library, equipment re-use programs, 3D printing, presentations, and other activities to help increase access to devices that help people do the activities they want and need to do in their lives.

Why?

A group of high schoolers realized that our local community projects needed more support to make their work more impactful and improve our lives.

1

Our Community

We believe that our community is one of the most important things to us, as it gives us a safe sense of home and surrounds us in our day-to-day lives. As residents of our community, we decided to find a way to give back to it, by fundraising for impactful local initiatives.

2

Collective Initiative

We believe that taking initiative is one of the most important skills to have in the modern world. Initiative is what drives us to achieve goals, which, in our case, means supporting our community through active fundraising.

3

Fundraising

Money is a key aspect of not only our lives, but also the main requirement of many local projects that support the community through their initiatives. Our goal is to help provide such local initiatives with the capital they need to continue making our community a better place.

4

Volunteering

We believe that volunteering is a crucial way to give back to the community by helping people and supporting causes that improve it. We deeply appreciate our volunteers who make our organization's fundraising efforts possible.
